Altoids Flashlight

Instructions for making an Altoids tin flashlight

Materials

  1. 1 Altoids tin with 3 holes drilled
  2. 1 Breadboard
  3. 2 Male to female jumper wires (Black)
  4. 1 Female to female jumper wire (Red)
  5. 1 9V battery clip connecter
  6. 2 LED holder mounts (LED holder mount, washer, and nut)
  7. 2 Colored 5mm LEDs
  8. 2 Clear 5mm LEDs
  9. 1 Micro push button swich

Instructions

Get out your LED holder mounts and the parts you see including the colored or clear LEDs.

Slip the clear plastic piece shown onto the LED. Repeat on other LED.

Slide the LED and the plastic piece, still on the bottom of LED, through the bottom of the LED holder mount. Repeat on other one.

Place the LED system you created into one of the 2 drilled holes on the top of the Altoids tin. Repeat with other LED system.

Slide the washer onto the LED holder mount(from the inside) and screw on the nut after placing the washer. Repeat on other LED holder mount configuration.

Take the breadboard and place the wires of the 9V battery clip connecter. Red, in the 1st (farthest from numbers) hole in the column to the right of the 3rd column. Black, in the 1st hole in the column to the left of the 1st column.
